Lava Cake

Ingredients

Chocolate, Butter, Eggs, Sugar, Flour, Cocoa powder, Salt

Preparation

Melt the chocolate and butter together using a double boiler. Beat the eggs and sugar together, then add the chocolate and remaining ingredients. Bake until the sides are set but the center is jiggly.

Serving

For a gorgeous presentation, dust these lava cakes with powdered sugar and serve them alongside bright red fruits (such as strawberries or raspberries). A scoop of vanilla ice cream and a drizzle of chocolate sauce will take things over the top.
